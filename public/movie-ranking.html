Movie Ranking Project

My top 3 movies of all-time

"The Lord of the Rings"

"The Lord of the Rings" is a 2001 American epic fantasy film directed by Peter Jackson based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novel of the same name. The film stars Elijah Wood and Viggo Mortensen as Frodo Baggins and Aragorn, respectively. The movie tells the story of Frodo, a hobbit who inherits a powerful ring from his uncle Bilbo and embarks on a perilous journey to destroy it before it falls into the hands of the dark lord Sauron.

"Clue"

"Clue" is a 1985 American comedy-mystery film directed by Jonathan Lynn and starring Tim Curry, Peter Falk, and Jeff Daniels. The film is a modern update of the classic board game "Clue" and follows the story of the game's characters as they try to solve a murder and uncover the identity of the killer. The movie was filmed in front of a live audience and features a number of famous actors and musicians as guests on the game show.

"Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron"

"Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron" is a 2002 American animated Western film directed by Kelly Asbury and starring John Turturro and Jennifer Aniston. The film is based on the 1970s comic book series "Spirit of the West" and follows the story of a wild mustang named Spirit who is captured by a group of cowboys and taken to a racetrack. Spirit then escapes and sets out on a journey to find his freedom and freedom for other wild horses.
